style(ui): unify modal design and harden interactive button primitives

Completed the Saffron & Obsidian migration for global modal systems and overhauled the button interaction engine for precise visual feedback.

Key Changes:
- Button Interaction Engine: Replaced `heroui-native` with standard `Pressable` in `button.tsx`, utilizing React state (`onPressIn/Out`) to ensure the new `primaryLight` saffron shade flashes correctly during interaction (fixing a transparent-style bug with the previous HOC).
- Theme Extension: Added the `primaryLight` token (`#E27E5A`) to `theme.ts` to provide a mathematically consistent lighter tint for active button states.
- Modal Hardening: Fully tokenized `ConfirmationModal`, mapping backgrounds to `surfaceElevated`, primary actions to Saffron, and typography to `textMain`/`textMuted`.
- Dropdown Polish: Darkened the `ActionMenu` (3-dots popup) to use the `surface` token, increased icon/text scale for better readability, and restored the subtle `surfaceLight` border.
- Typography Scaling: Scaled up global confirmation titles to `text-xl` to improve the visual hierarchy during destructive actions.

This work ensures that even the most subtle micro-interactions—like a button press or a dropdown menu—are strictly governed by the central design system.
