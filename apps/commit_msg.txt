CRITICAL FIX:
- Implemented `ConnectionWatchdog` to resolve the "Zombie WebSocket" bug observed
  after extended Android deep sleep (6+ hours).
- The watchdog probes connection health on foreground resume and forces a 
  WebSocket resurrection via `clearAuth`/`getSession` if a stale state or 
  mutation timeout is detected. This eliminates the need for phone restarts 
  to restore write capabilities.
