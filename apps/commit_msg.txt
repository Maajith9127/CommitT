refactor(native): isolate preset management into a dedicated route group

Reorganized the route structure to decouple preset library management from
the primary commitment creation flow. This ensures a distinct visual identity
for editing presets and prevents route pollution in the create-commit stack.

- Established the `(edit-preset)` group with a dedicated `_layout.tsx`.
- Implemented a smooth 'fade' transition for the entire preset management flow
  to visually separate "Library Management" from "Active Flow".
- Migrated the following screens to the new group:
    - `edit-digital-preset.tsx`
    - `edit-location-preset.tsx`
    - `search-preset-location.tsx`
- Refactored `presets.tsx` to point navigation to the new `/(edit-preset)` paths.
- Updated root `app/_layout.tsx` stack to support the new navigation group.
- Cleaned up redundant files from the `(create-commit)` directory.
