feat(ui/ux): harden accountability guards and unify design system typography

Implemented a robust, state-based security layer for commitment deletion and 
standardized typography Across the Presets Hub and Navigation layers.

Accountability & Deletion Security:
- Hardened `deleteInstance` logic to prevent deletion during 'pending', 'proceeding', 
  'waiver_active', or 'penalized' states when Strict Mode is active.
- Integrated `ConfirmationModal` into the deletion and calendar-resize flows 
  to provide friendly "Vault Active" feedback with dynamic unlock timestamps.
- Silenced system-level logging for expected `STRICT_LOCK_ACTIVE` constraints to 
  reduce console noise during valid domain-logic blocks.
- Polished `CaptchaWaiverView` error handling, mapping expired sessions to 
  clean UI messages instead of raw server errors.

Typography & Design System:
- Refactored `presets.tsx` to replace raw React Native text with centralized 
  `HeaderTitle`, `BodyText`, and `FooterText` components.
- Adjusted Preset Card typography: increased title sizing to `text-lg` and 
  standardized subtexts for better information hierarchy.
- Updated `BottomBar.tsx` to use `HeaderTitle` for tab labels with a high-contrast 
  color palette (Brand Blue for focused state, Pure White for inactive state).
