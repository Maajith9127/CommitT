fix(enforce): prioritize hardware alarms over blocker to prevent UI deadlock

- Implemented a "Global Alarm Mutex" via SharedPreferences to synchronize state between the Scheduler and Blocker native modules.
- Refactored `AlarmActivity` to broadcast its active state ($is_alarm_active) and updated its Manifest to `singleInstance` with unique `taskAffinity` for absolute window stack isolation.
- Modified `BlockerAccessibilityService` to enter "Silent Passthrough" mode when the alarm mutex is active, suspending all enforcement and preventing overlay launches.
- Added a failsafe to `BlockerOverlayActivity` to immediately `finish()` if an alarm is detected in `onCreate`, ensuring the Dismiss button is never obscured.

This resolves a critical safety issue where concurrent blocker triggers could bury the alarm UI, resulting in infinite ringing and requiring a device restart.
