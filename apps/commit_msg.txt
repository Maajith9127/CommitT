fix: resolve slot-specific behavioral rule persistence bug

Stabilized the full data lifecycle for per-slot behavioral settings 
(alarms, grace periods, etc.) by aligning the frontend store with 
the backend schema and persistence orchestration.

Key changes:
- Schema: Added 'ruleConfig' to the 'time_windows' definition for 
  both 'tasks' and 'taskInstances' in Convex.
- API: Updated 'RecurrenceSchema' validators to permit 'ruleConfig' 
  during commitment creation and updates.
- Orchestration: Fixed 'useCommitTask' hook to explicitly serialize 
  and send 'ruleConfig' in the payload, ensuring it reaches the database.
- Hydration: Implemented an auto-hydration fallback in 'useTaskDraftStore' 
  to reconstruct 'ruleConfig' from 'config' when loading existing tasks.

This eliminates the "blank UI" bug where slot-specific rules disappeared 
after saving or reloading a commitment.
