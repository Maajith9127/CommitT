feat(native/blocker): add background location permission to enforcer manifest

Resolves the "False Positive" block issue where the enforcement engine would
trigger the blocker UI when the app was closed or the screen was off. 

By adding ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION, the BlockerAccessibilityService can now 
maintain its 1Hz GPS stream in the background, preventing the Fail-Closed 
logic from triggering due to "Null" or stale location data when the device 
is locked.

BC: Users must now grant "Allow all the time" in Android system settings.
