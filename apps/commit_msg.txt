fix(verification): exclude digital commitments from discrete pulse-check status

- Bug: Task instances were adding "digital_commitment: pending" to discrete location checkpoints during updates.
- Logic: Digital commitments are violation-based (continuous) or auto-verified, and do not require point-in-time pulse checks like location rules.
- Fix: Updated `generateStayThroughoutCheckpoints` and `generateJustShowUpCheckpoints` to skip 'digital_commitment' when populating the `verification_status` of a checkpoint.
- Impact: Corrects verification reporting and ensures that discrete check-in windows correctly focus only on anchor-based conditions (location).
