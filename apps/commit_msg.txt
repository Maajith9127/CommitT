feat(core): implement contextual slot-specific condition overrides

This major architectural upgrade enables per-slot configuration for location 
and digital blocklist rules, allowing users to define different accountability 
contracts for different times within the same recurring habit.

Key Changes:
- Schema: Updated `RecurrenceSchema` in `lib/validators` to include optional 
  `conditions` for each time window in the task recurrence manifest.
- Store: Extended `useTaskDraftStore` with `setSlotLocation` and `setSlotBlocklist` 
  actions to persist per-window configurations within the global draft state.
- UI: Refactored `time-set.tsx` to eliminate local state in favor of the unified 
  Zustand store. `TimeSlotCard` components now reactively derive labels 
  (e.g., specific addresses or app counts) from the slot's condition manifest.
- Engine: Updated `Instances.generateSeries` with a new `mergeConditions` utility. 
  It implements a "Contextual Override" strategy where slot-level rules take 
  precedence over global task rules during instance expansion.
- Logic: Pushed slot conditions through the `generateTimeSlots` horizon engine 
  and synchronized internal domain types (`GeneratedSlot`, `TimeWindow`) to 
  ensure end-to-end type safety.

This ensures that the generated hardware-level verification checkpoints are 
always contextually accurate to the specific time window being enforced.
